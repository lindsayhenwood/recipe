Lindsay's Favorite Mountain Bowl Recipe

Welcome to one of the best apres-ski meals out there. Meant to nutrify your body and fill your stomach, you will be ready for anything after this healthy feast.

Ingredients for the Bowl

    • 3 Cups of Spinach
    • 1 Cup Brown Rice
    • 1/2 Cup Almonds
    • Goat Cheese
    • 1 Avocado
    • 2 Beets
    • 2 Carrots

Ingredients for the Dressing

    • 1/3 Cup Tahini
    • 1/3 Cup Olive Oil
    • 1/3 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar
    • 1/2 Cup Nutritional Yeast
    • 1/3 Cup Soy Sauce
    • 2 Cloves of Garlic
    • 1/3 Cup Water

Directions

  1. Cook a cup of brown rice and let it cool a little.
  2. Chop up the almonds, grate the beets and carrots and cut the avocado into slices.
  3. Throw all of the dressing ingredients into a blender. Blend until smooth.
  4. Once the rice has cooled, put an equal amount into each of the bowls. Then top with the grated carrots and beets, spinach, and avocado.
  5. Sprinkle the chopped almonds and crumble the goat cheese.
  6. Finally, drizzle the dressing on top.

Enjoy your Mountain Bowl!
